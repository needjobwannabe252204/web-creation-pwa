Topic 3: Colors

Why Colors Matter.

Colors play a crucial role in web design, as they can influence user emotions, highlight important elements, and improve the overall visual appeal of a website.


Color Common Meaning
Red Passion, Energy, Urgency
Blue Trust, Calmness, Professionalism
Green Growth, Health, Nature
Yellow Happiness, Optimism, Attention-Grabbing


Color Psychology in Web Design

Different colors can evoke different emotions and reactions from users. For example, red can create a sense of urgency, making it effective for call-to-action buttons. Blue can instill trust and is often used by financial institutions. Green is associated with health and nature, making it popular for eco-friendly brands. Yellow can grab attention and create a cheerful atmosphere.
