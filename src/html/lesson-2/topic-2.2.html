Topic 2: Headings and Paragraphs

Headings & Paragraph

Headings

Headings organize information and create titles and subtitles.

Best practice: use one <h1> per page and follow heading order (<h1> to <h6>) to preserve document structure and accessibility.


Paragraphs

Paragraphs display blocks of text.


HTML Headings & Paragraphs:

Tag Output
<h1> </h1>

Heading 1
<h2> </h2>

Heading 2
<h3> </h3>

Heading 3
<h4> </h4>

Heading 4
<h5> </h5>
Heading 5
<h6> </h6>
Heading 6
<p> </p>

Paragraph


Example

<h1>About Me</h1>

<p>My name is Juan.</p>
<p>I enjoy learning web development.</p>

Result

About Me

My name is Juan.

I enjoy learning web development.
